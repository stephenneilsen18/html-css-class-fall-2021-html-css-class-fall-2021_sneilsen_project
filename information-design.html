Stories that don’t resonate aren’t worth telling. Data is not worth showing if the conclusions are not immediately evidentto the viewer. The best communcators are teachers who can advance the most powerful tool—a great story. When a story is told in a compelling way, the result is increased understanding and empathy from listeners. Among the most effective ways to produce that understanding is displaying information both visually and thematically, and all infographics worth their salt do both.
