Illustrations

Sometimes, the nature of the idea or concept you're trying to convey will determine whether illustration is the best course of action. Abstract themes, or topics where specific visuals don't come to mind, will often lend themselves better to an illustrative treatment. Illustration can also be a powerful way to customize imagry to meet specific aims or augment the appearance of photographed imagery. My Illustrations have been used in a variety of contexts, from infographics, advertisements, digital assets and interfaces, and logo design.
